Smoothie

Description

Banana, quick oats, peanut butter, frozen fruit, milk, salt, and cinnamon all added together make for a good smoothie. Use a ninja blender. And add protein powder, I like chocolate.

Ingredients

Makes 1 serving for 200lb athlete

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • half a spoonful or so of peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup to 1 cup of frozen fruit
  • milk to the liquid line
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • salt
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Measure and put in blender
  2. blend
  3. done
